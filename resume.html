About Me

I do a variety of freelance work, working on projects with incredibly varied subjects and styles.

I currently work as a multimedia producer for the Carnegie Corporation of New York, creating multimedia stories about the foundation's grantees.

Prior to that, I was a freelance video producer for two years. I worked on a range of projects during that time, including a video about a comedian for The New Yorker, a series of videos for Sotheby's called Behind the Collector, and short video profile of a cam girl for Discovery Digital. I'm currently collaborating on an interactive documentary focusing on stories of sexual assault.

I grew up in California's Mojave Desert, but I now call New York City home.

If I could take all the bodega cats home with me, I would.
