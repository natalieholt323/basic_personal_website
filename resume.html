About Me

I do a variety of freelance work, working on projects with incredibly varied subjects and styles.

I currently work as a multimedia producer for the Carnegie Corporation of New York, creating multimedia stories about the foundation's grantees.

Video Producing

Prior to Carnegie, I was a freelance video producer for two years. I worked on a range of projects during that time, including a video about a comedian for The New Yorker, a series of videos for Sotheby's called Behind the Collector, a short video profile of a cam girl for Discovery Digital, and a series of short videos for the just launched 12 Kinds of Kindness. I'm currently collaborating on an interactive documentary focusing on stories of sexual assault.
